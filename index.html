When you logout the page is refreshed.
There are four ways a user can leave an application
  • Press "Logout" - total control by application.
  • Press "F5" - total control by application, preventDefault on keydown.
  • Press Refresh button - beforeunload will allow you to show a browser dialog.
  • Press "X" window button - beforeunload will allow you to show a browser dialog.
